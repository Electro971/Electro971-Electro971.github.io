About the Bloodgrounds

The Blaviken Bloodgrounds is the infamous home of the Blaviken Bloodhounds, renowned across the league for its raucous crowds, crimson-stained turf, and legendary atmosphere. Built on the bones of ancient battlefields, the stadium is a fortress where only the bravest dare to play.

  • Capacity: 35,000 screaming fans
  • Location: Outskirts of Blaviken, beside the River Yaruga
  • Opened: 2472
  • Surface: Reinforced grass (with extra padding for blood stains)

Stadium Features

  • Bloodweiser End Zone – Official sponsor and fan favorite refreshment area
  • The Skull Stand – Reserved for the most passionate (and loudest) supporters
  • Hall of Maul – On-site museum of Bloodhounds’ greatest moments
  • State-of-the-art medical bay (for both players and unlucky fans)

Visiting the Bloodgrounds

Tickets sell out fast, so secure your seat early! Whether you’re a die-hard Bloodhound or a brave visitor, the Blaviken Bloodgrounds promises an unforgettable matchday experience. Remember: “Blood for the Blood God, Touchdowns for the Team!”
