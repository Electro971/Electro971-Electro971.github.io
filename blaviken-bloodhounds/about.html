About the Bloodhounds

The Blaviken Bloodhounds are a legendary Blood Bowl team, renowned for their ferocity, tenacity, and unyielding thirst for victory (and occasionally, for blood). Founded in the heart of Blaviken, the team has become a symbol of strength and determination, both on and off the pitch.

Our motto, “Blood for the Blood God, Touchdowns for the Team!”, reflects our commitment to thrilling play and unforgettable moments. Whether it’s a last-minute touchdown or a bone-crunching tackle, the Bloodhounds never fail to entertain their loyal fans.

Our Mission

  • Deliver exhilarating Blood Bowl action every match.
  • Foster a passionate and inclusive fan community.
  • Honor the traditions of Blaviken and the sport.
  • Support our players on and off the field.
