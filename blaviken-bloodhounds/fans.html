Coach Shuler Promises "More Skulls, Less Fumbles" This Season

Date: 5 Sept 2025

In a recent interview, Coach Shuler of the Blaviken Bloodhounds outlined his ambitious plans for the upcoming season, emphasizing a focus on aggressive play and minimizing errors.

"More skulls, less fumbles."
Shuler declared, highlighting his commitment to enhancing the team's performance on the field. Shuler discussed the importance of physicality in Blood Bowl, stating that the team will prioritize inflicting casualties on their opponents while maintaining solid ball control. He believes that a more ruthless approach will not only intimidate rival teams but also energize the Bloodhounds' passionate fan base. The coach also touched on the need for improved training and discipline among players, aiming to reduce turnovers and capitalize on scoring opportunities.
"We need to be smarter with the ball," he noted, "but we also need to be fiercer in our tackles."
Fans are eagerly anticipating how these strategies will unfold in the coming matches, hoping that Shuler's vision will lead to a successful season filled with victories and memorable moments.

Blaviken Council Pleased with Win, Displeased with Fan Wildness

Date: 5 Sept 2025

The Blaviken town council has expressed mixed feelings following the Blaviken Bloodhounds' recent victory over the Norsca Rampagers. While the council is pleased with the team's performance and the positive impact on local morale, they have raised concerns about the behavior of some fans during and after the match. Council members noted that while passionate support is appreciated, certain actions crossed the line into unruly conduct, including excessive noise levels, minor property damage, and confrontations with opposing fans. The council is urging all supporters to celebrate responsibly and uphold the community's reputation. In response, the Bloodhounds' management has pledged to work closely with local authorities to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for all attendees at future games. They are also considering implementing additional measures to promote positive fan engagement and sportsmanship.

Bloodhounds Win Opening Match of the Season!

Date: 4 Sept 2025

The Blaviken Bloodhounds kicked off their 2025 season with a hard-fought victory against the Norsca Rampagers, winning 1-0 in a match that kept fans on the edge of their seats. The lone touchdown was scored early in the first half, setting the tone for a game filled with intense physicality and strategic plays. The Bloodhounds' defence held strong throughout the second half, successfully repelling numerous attacks from the Rampagers. The team's ability to inflict a total of eight casualties on their opponents further demonstrated their dominance on the field. Fans erupted in cheers as the final whistle blew, celebrating a victory that not only boosts team morale but also sets a positive trajectory for the rest of the season. Coach Shuler praised his players for their resilience and teamwork, promising "more skulls, less fumbles" in upcoming matches.
